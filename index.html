Turn the Tide | Together

Turn the Tide is a non-profit designed to help women with small children or infants attain transitional housing after completion of a 90 day Substance Abuse Treatment Program. Evidence-based research shows that person's electing to receive extra supportive services have a 70% greater success rate. We define success as maintaining abstinence from mind altering substances, developing a supportive environment and actively working a recovery program continuously for 1 year.

Your donations will provide rent, food, hygienes, bus tickets, etc for one woman and one child. Our scholarships pay up to the first 90 days of transitional housing at one of the recovery homes listed below. Please check out the links and visit their websites.
